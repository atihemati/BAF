Balmorel-Antares Soft-Linking Framework

The purpose of this document is to provide a practical guide on how to use the Balmorel-Antares Soft-Linking Framework (BAF). For a scientific application, see Rosendal et al[1]. BAF is still at a very early stage of development, meaning this relatively scarce documentation will be extended over time.

[1]

Rosendal, M., J. Janin, T. Heggarty, D. Pisinger, R. Bramstoft, and M. Münster. “The Benefits and Challenges of Soft-Linking Investment and Operational Energy System Models.” Applied Energy 385 (May 1, 2025): 125512. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2025.125512.
